## Scoring Framework

Each company is scored 0–100 against their own published climate pledges.
Score = weighted composite of verified performance across four indicator categories.

### Four Categories (equal weight, 25 points each)

| Category | What Is Measured |
|---|---|
| Emissions Trajectory | Scope 1+2+3 actual vs required reduction path |
| Pledge Integrity | Specificity, verification, target year validity |
| Reporting Transparency | CDP disclosure, third-party audit, Scope 3 coverage |
| Structural Commitment | Capital allocation, business model alignment |

### Score Bands
- 0–30: BREACH — emissions moving wrong direction or pledge unverifiable
- 31–60: WARNING — partial progress, material gaps vs stated path
- 61–84: DRIFTING — on partial path, structural gaps remain
- 85–100: ON TRACK — verified trajectory consistent with stated pledge

## BP — Score: 11 / 100
### BREACH

**Pledge:** Net zero by 2050. 35–40% reduction in oil and gas production by 2030 vs 2019.

**Verified Reality (FY2024 data · CDP 2023 · SEC 20-F 2024):**

| Indicator | Required by Now | Actual FY2024 | Gap |
|---|---|---|---|
| Production reduction | −10% vs 2019 (linear path) | +3% vs 2019 | −13 pts |
| Scope 1+2 emissions | −15% vs 2019 | −8% vs 2019 | −7 pts |
| Scope 3 emissions | Reduction trajectory | Not reported with 2030 target | Material gap |
| Renewable investment % | >20% capex | ~15% capex | Below path |

**Emissions Trajectory: 4/25**
Production has increased vs 2019 baseline. On a linear path to 35% reduction by 2030, BP should be at approximately −10% now. Actual: +3%. Gap: 13 percentage points moving the wrong direction.

**Pledge Integrity: 2/25**
BP revised its 2030 production reduction target in February 2023 from 35–40% to 25%, then in February 2025 announced it was abandoning the production reduction target entirely and increasing oil and gas investment. Published pledge is no longer operative.

**Reporting Transparency: 3/25**
CDP Climate questionnaire submitted. Scope 3 disclosure incomplete for downstream use of sold products at granular target level. 20-F filed with SEC.

**Structural Commitment: 2/25**
February 2025 strategic reset explicitly increased fossil fuel capital allocation. No structural mechanism announced to reach net zero by 2050 under current investment trajectory.

**Total: 11/100 — BREACH**

## Open Proof Protocol

Every score in CONSCIENCE is:
- Derived from public data only (CDP, SEC, company reports, satellite)
- Challengeable by anyone with a source citation
- Corrected publicly and permanently when an error is found
