## What Reviewers Are Evaluating

- Scientific validity of the 8-system planetary boundary framework
- Accuracy of the 48 indicator selection and data sources
- Integrity of the Deviation-from-Baseline Weighted Composite Index formula
- Appropriateness of the pre-industrial 1850–1900 baseline
- Soundness of the uncertainty propagation (±1.8 points)
- Alignment with IPCC AR6, Steffen et al. (2015), and Richardson et al. (2023)
- Validity of CONSCIENCE scoring methodology (CDP, InfluenceMap, Sabin Center, SBTi)

Reviewers are not asked to endorse VYRION as an organization or its commercial activities.
Reviewers are asked only to assess whether the methodology is scientifically sound.

---

## Open Proof Protocol

VYRION publishes all corrections openly. If a reviewer identifies an error:

1. Email contact@vyrion.earth with the specific error and the correct information
2. VYRION publishes the correction within 24 hours
3. The correction is logged in the public GitHub record

Corrections strengthen the methodology. They are never hidden.
