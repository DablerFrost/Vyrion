# DECISION-GRADE INTELLIGENCE

VYRION describes certain outputs as **decision-grade**.

Within the VYRION ecosystem, decision-grade means:

* Methodologies are publicly documented
* Source materials are identified whenever possible
* Calculations are reproducible
* Scores can be independently reviewed
* Updates and corrections are traceable
* Analytical frameworks remain open to scrutiny

Decision-grade does not mean infallible.

No benchmark, dataset, research platform, analytical model, or intelligence system can guarantee perfect accuracy.

Decision-grade means VYRION is designed to support informed decisions through transparent evidence and accountable methodology.
