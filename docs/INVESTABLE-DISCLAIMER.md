## 1. What "Decision-Grade" and "Investable" Mean in Context

The VYRION Index is described as a **decision-grade planetary health benchmark** — meaning it is designed to meet the data quality, transparency, reproducibility, and auditability standards required for institutional use in research, risk assessment, and investment due diligence.

The term **"investable"** as applied to the VYRION Index means the same thing it means for any benchmark index (S&P 500, MSCI World, Bloomberg Commodity Index): the index measures something real, systematic, and quantifiable, and can in principle serve as a basis for financial instruments or investment decisions. It does not mean:

- That VYRION PBC provides investment advice
- That VYRION recommends any specific investment action
- That the VYRION Index is a registered financial product or regulated benchmark
- That VYRION PBC is a registered investment advisor, broker-dealer, or financial institution

## 2. Not Investment Advice

**The VYRION Index, VYRION CONSCIENCE scores, and all related data and analytics are informational benchmarks. They are not investment advice.**

Specifically, VYRION does not:
- Recommend buying, selling, or holding any security, fund, or financial instrument
- Provide personalized financial advice to any individual or institution
- Make representations about the future performance of any asset, company, or market
- Act as a fiduciary in any capacity

## 3. Institutional Use Guidance

### Appropriate use of VYRION data in investment contexts

VYRION scores are appropriate inputs for:
- ESG due diligence and screening processes
- Climate risk research and scenario analysis
- Portfolio-level sustainability assessment
- Academic and policy research on corporate climate accountability
- Journalism and public interest reporting on corporate climate performance

### Required independent due diligence

Institutional investors, fund managers, analysts, and any party incorporating VYRION data into a financial process must:
- Conduct independent due diligence on all companies and securities
- Verify VYRION-cited source data directly from primary sources
- Apply their own judgment and investment process to VYRION analytical outputs
- Consult qualified financial, legal, and compliance advisors before making investment decisions

VYRION scores are one input among many in a proper investment due diligence process. They are not a substitute for full financial analysis.

## 6. Forward-Looking Statements

VYRION scores reflect current measured conditions against a historical baseline. They are not predictions of future planetary conditions, corporate behavior, or market outcomes. Any institutional use of VYRION data to support forward-looking analysis, scenario modeling, or stress testing is the user's own analytical work and is not endorsed or validated by VYRION.

## 7. Score Limitations Relevant to Financial Use

Institutional users should be aware of:

- **Annual indicator latency:** 20 of 48 VYRION Index indicators update annually. Point-in-time scores may not reflect developments within the current reporting year.
- **CONSCIENCE score timing:** Corporate scores update on source publication cycles (CDP annual, SBTi periodic, Sabin Center ongoing). Scores between update events reflect the most recently verified data.
- **Uncertainty bounds:** The ±1.8 point VYRION Index uncertainty represents documented measurement error. Day-to-day changes below ±0.5 are within measurement noise.
- **Coverage scope:** CONSCIENCE currently covers 11 companies. This is not a comprehensive market index and does not represent a defined investable universe.
