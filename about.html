01 · Thesis

Not activism. Not a nonprofit. Accountability infrastructure.

Existing ESG ratings measure what companies report. ISS ESG covers more than 10,000 issuers; MSCI covers more than 16,000. Both products are dominated by self-reported disclosures, and both have been the subject of repeated academic and regulatory critique for measuring intention rather than impact.

VYRION measures the gap between what companies pledge and what verifiable public data shows they are doing — across CDP disclosures, InfluenceMap lobbying alignment grades, the Sabin Center climate litigation database, the Science Based Targets initiative registry, and live SEC EDGAR regulatory filings. Every score is source-cited, every correction is published within 24 hours, and every change is permanently logged. We call this the Open Proof Protocol.

The thesis is that institutional capital does not need a better ESG survey. It needs a real-time, source-cited benchmark — the kind of infrastructure that climate science has never built and that the financial markets have always required to price risk.

02 · The Stack

Four layers, two live products.

VYRION ships in layers, each independently auditable.

CONSCIENCE
Corporate climate pledge tracker. 11 companies scored 0–100 on a four-factor framework (Pledge Quality 30 · CDP 25 · Lobbying Alignment 25 · Legal Exposure 20). Methodology v1.1 locked.

VYRION Index
Daily 0–100 planetary health benchmark anchored to a pre-industrial 1850–1900 baseline. 48 indicators across 8 Earth systems (Atmosphere, Ocean, Cryosphere, Forest, Biodiversity, Freshwater, Soil, Corporate Accountability). Source: NOAA, NASA, NSIDC, Copernicus, GBIF, FAO.
Live
Bio-Digital Twin
Causal attribution engine linking corporate Scope 1–3 emissions and supply chain activity to specific planetary boundary breaches. Per-company Liability Score grounded in Steffen et al. (2015), Richardson et al. (2023), and PIK Potsdam.

API layer for Boundary-Linked Bonds, Regenerative Credits, Earth Stability Credits, and Cross-Boundary Value-at-Risk. Smart-contract triggers tied to live boundary thresholds. Architecture defined; regulatory framework pending.

04 · Method

Open Proof Protocol.

Every score VYRION publishes is derived from public-domain or open-license data. Every source is linked from the live platform. The methodology is versioned and locked — any change increments the version number, is committed to a public Git history (github.com/DablerFrost/Vyrion), and is recorded in an on-page changelog that never deletes prior entries.

If you find an error in any score, source citation, or formula, report it to [email protected]. We will publish a correction within 24 hours as a permanent, timestamped entry in the Score Changelog. Original entries are never deleted; they are superseded with full attribution and dated diff.

This is the institutional credibility primitive that legacy ESG products do not have.
