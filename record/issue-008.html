Key Findings

Finding 01 — The only verified ON TRACK company in this portfolio. Apple scores 84/100 — the highest CONSCIENCE score across all 11 tracked companies, and the only profile built primarily on verified outcomes rather than forward pledges. A 62% absolute emission reduction since 2015 is documented in Apple EPR 2024 and independently confirmed through CDP 2023 filings. This is not a reporting exercise. The reductions are real.

Finding 02 — Supply chain penetration is the structural differentiator. The Supplier Clean Energy Program — covering >80% of manufacturing energy — solves the problem that collapses Scope 3 alignment for most companies in this portfolio. Apple has moved verifiably into its supply chain, not just its owned operations. This is the hardest part of decarbonization for any global manufacturer, and it is where Apple's score separates from every Warning and Breach company tracked here.

Finding 03 — The one structural gap: InfluenceMap B+. Apple's CDP grade is A. Its SBTi status is Approved 1.5°C. Its legal exposure is zero. But InfluenceMap grades Apple at B+, not A — representing 19 of a possible 25 points. This gap is attributable to policy engagement that is broadly aligned but not uniformly active across all trade association affiliations and jurisdictions. If Apple's lobbying alignment closes to A-level, the CONSCIENCE score moves to 90/100. That shift is trackable. VYRION will publish it when it happens.

Finding 04 — Legal score at 10/20 despite zero lawsuits. Apple is the only company in this portfolio with no active climate litigation in the Sabin Center database — a clean legal record at global scale. The 10/20 reflects structural scrutiny factors: EU Digital Markets Act and ESG disclosure compliance exposure, mounting sector-wide litigation risk as mandatory climate disclosure frameworks tighten globally, and regulatory surface area at Apple's scale of operation. Zero cases today does not mean zero structural exposure.

Capital Markets Signal

Apple at 84/100 is the highest verified sustainability profile in CONSCIENCE's current portfolio. The investable signal is structural: Apple has built verifiable planetary accountability infrastructure into its supply chain at scale — a competitive moat that strengthens as carbon disclosure mandates and pricing mechanisms tighten globally.

The 62% absolute emission reduction since 2015 — verified across CDP, SEC EDGAR, and the SBTi registry — demonstrates trajectory, not intent. A 2030 full value chain carbon neutrality target, validated by Science Based Targets initiative, converts to a 4-year execution window against an already-compressed baseline. The Supplier Clean Energy Program at >80% penetration signals that the hardest part of the execution — supply chain transformation — is underway with measurable progress.

The gap to monitor: InfluenceMap B+ versus A standard. The 6-point lobbying gap is the only remaining structural constraint on Apple's CONSCIENCE score. It is a single, trackable variable.

Investable Signal: Strongest verified decarbonization trajectory in this portfolio. Lowest transition risk exposure. Highest regulatory alignment. Watch for: InfluenceMap grade improvement; EPR 2025 Supplier Clean Energy penetration update; EDGAR disclosure of 2025 interim milestone status.
