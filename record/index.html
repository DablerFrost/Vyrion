Scoring Methodology
Every Record issue derives its CONSCIENCE score from four weighted public data sources: Pledge Quality (30 pts), CDP Performance (25 pts), InfluenceMap Lobbying Alignment (25 pts), and Legal Exposure via Sabin Center (20 pts). All sources are linked directly inside each issue. Full derivation: CONSCIENCE-METHODOLOGY.md →
Open Proof Protocol
Every score in every issue is challengeable. Find an error — contact@vyrion.earth — and VYRION publishes the correction within 24 hours. Corrections are additive and public. Originals are never deleted. This protocol applies to every issue, every score, every data point, permanently.
Publication Standard
No issue publishes without a confirmed CONSCIENCE-METHODOLOGY.md breakdown for that company. No score is stated without a named source. No pledge is quoted without a link to the original filing. The Record is a permanent public document, not a news cycle. It does not expire.
