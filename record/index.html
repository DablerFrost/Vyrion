Scoring Methodology
How CONSCIENCE Scores Are Derived
Each CONSCIENCE score is computed from four weighted public data sources, cross-referenced against Paris Agreement 1.5°C pathways and Science Based Targets initiative criteria:

Pledge Quality (30 pts) — Ambition level, scope coverage (1/2/3), deadline, third-party validation (SBTi status). · CDP Performance (25 pts) — A=25 · B=18 · C=10 · D=4. Independent annual assessment. · InfluenceMap Lobbying Alignment (25 pts) — A+=25 → F=0. Penalises companies lobbying against the policies their pledges require. · Legal Exposure (20 pts) — Base 20 − (0.5 × active Sabin Center cases); floor 0.
