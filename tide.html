Hardware Philosophy
Modular · Solar · Self-reporting
All TIDE hardware units are designed around three principles: modularity (stackable capacity from village to city scale), solar-only power, and autonomous telemetry. Every unit publishes its operational data to ATLAS every 15 minutes.
AI Architecture
Open-weights · ATLAS-native · Auditable
TIDE's routing and optimization models are built on open-weights architecture. Every routing decision is logged with the input data that produced it. No black-box scoring. Full methodology available at VYRION-TIDE-METHODOLOGY-v1.0.md.
Water Purification Spec
AWG · RO · Graphene-oxide membrane
Dual system: atmospheric water generation for arid deployment, reverse osmosis with graphene-oxide enhanced membranes for contaminated source water. PFAS, heavy metal, and microplastic removal to WHO drinking water standards.
OAE Safety Protocol
Electrochemical · Reversible · Monitored
Ocean alkalinity enhancement via electrochemical splitting — no chemical additives, no irreversible processes. Every OAE unit operates within a continuous ATLAS-monitored safety envelope. Operations halt automatically if pH drift exceeds protocol bounds.
