📡 What Are the Signals?
📊
Real Data, Right Now!
VYRION shows actual measurements from satellites and sensors around the world — like a thermometer for the whole planet!
🌡️
CO₂ — Earth's Invisible Blanket
CO₂ is a gas. Too much of it, Earth gets extra warm — like too many blankets on a hot night! 433 ppm means it's getting quite warm.
🧊
Arctic Ice — Earth's Reflector
White ice reflects sunlight back into space! When ice melts, the dark ocean soaks up MORE heat. Losing your sun umbrella on a hot day. ☂️
🏢 What is CONSCIENCE?
🤝
Promises vs. Reality
Big companies sometimes promise to help the planet. CONSCIENCE checks if they're actually keeping those promises — using real satellite data, not just words. 🛸
🎯
ON TRACK vs BREACH
ON TRACK = company is doing what they promised! 🎉 BREACH = their actions don't match their words. Like saying you'll clean your room... and not cleaning it. 😅
🌟 Fun Facts to Wow Your Friends
🌍 If Earth were the size of an apple, its entire atmosphere — all the air we breathe — would be thinner than the apple's skin. That's exactly why we watch it so closely.
🌊 The ocean covers 71% of Earth's surface and absorbs 90% of the heat we've added to the planet. It's been protecting us for decades — and we track it every single day.
🌱 One big tree absorbs around 48 pounds of CO₂ per year. Every single tree counts — including the ones you might plant someday! 🌳
