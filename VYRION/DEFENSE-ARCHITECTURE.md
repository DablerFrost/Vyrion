**Six mechanisms:**
1. **Algorithm Codex** — all methodology published openly on GitHub
2. **VYRION Ledger** — immutable public record of every credit, measurement, and prediction
3. **Prediction Archive** — every SHIELD prediction timestamped before the event
4. **Science Council Veto** — binding authority to publish dissenting opinions publicly
5. **Adversarial Audit Network** — pay external scientists $10K–$100K to find flaws

## THE ANTIFRAGILITY PRINCIPLE

Every threat VYRION faces either:
1. **Requires secrecy to work** — Radical transparency neutralizes it automatically
2. **Requires central control** — Distributed sovereignty removes the target
3. **Requires proving the concept is weak** — The open methodology means the attack becomes a public scientific debate VYRION wins

**The bottom line:** An attack on VYRION's science becomes a public demonstration of VYRION's integrity. A data poisoning attempt that gets detected and published publicly becomes the most powerful possible demonstration of VYRION's security architecture.

**The system is designed to get stronger when things go wrong.**
