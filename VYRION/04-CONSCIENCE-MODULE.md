## WHAT IT IS

VYRION CONSCIENCE is a live, public, satellite-verified corporate climate pledge tracker. It monitors corporate environmental commitments against verified satellite data and public filings in real time. Every divergence between pledge and reality is logged to the public VYRION Ledger permanently — timestamped, hashed, and immutable.

96% of pledging companies exhibit at least one greenwashing risk indicator (Nature Climate Action, Feb 2026). Only 5% of Forbes 2000 companies meet credible net-zero criteria (Net Zero Tracker 2024). Corporate climate pledges fail because there is no real-time, public, satellite-verified accountability mechanism.

CONSCIENCE is that mechanism.

---

## HOW IT WORKS

**Step 1 — Pledge ingestion**
Corporate pledges are sourced verbatim from CDP annual climate questionnaire responses, SEC mandatory climate disclosures, and official company sustainability/annual reports. No paraphrasing. Exact quotes with source citations.

**Step 2 — Trajectory calculation**
Required reduction curves use linear interpolation from base year to target year, benchmarked against SBTi 1.5°C pathway requirements.

**Step 3 — Satellite verification**
ESA Sentinel-5P / TROPOMI provides daily atmospheric methane readings at 5.5km resolution. Global Forest Watch provides near-real-time deforestation alerts at 30m resolution. These independent signals cross-reference self-reported corporate data.

**Step 4 — CONSCIENCE Score**
0–100 composite score reflecting pledge ambition, trajectory accuracy, scope coverage, and methodology transparency.

**Step 5 — Public Ledger**
Every scan result, every data ingestion, every flag is written to the immutable VYRION public Ledger. Timestamped. Hash-verified. Permanent.
