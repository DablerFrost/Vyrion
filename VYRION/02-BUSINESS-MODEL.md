## FOUR REVENUE TIERS

### Tier 1 — B2G · Governments
Annual module licensing to cities, regions, and nations. Compliance reporting tools. Disaster intelligence contracts. Integration with national environmental agencies.

**Deal size:** $2M–$50M per contract
**Sales cycle:** 18–36 months
**First target:** Small island nations (Tuvalu, Maldives, Vanuatu) — fastest moving, most motivated, immune to fossil fuel lobbying

### Tier 2 — B2B · Enterprise
ESG compliance platforms, supply chain environmental tracking, CONSCIENCE verification badge licensing, corporate sustainability dashboards.

**Deal size:** $80K–$2M per year
**Sales cycle:** 30–90 days for data licenses · 3–6 months for full platform
**First targets:** Insurance and reinsurance firms (Swiss Re, Munich Re, Lloyd's) — data-hungry, fast procurement, immediate need for SHIELD and ATLAS data

### Tier 3 — Impact Marketplace
VYRION takes 3–8% transaction fee on all verified impact credits traded through PULSE. Carbon, biodiversity, water, and soil credits.

**Revenue model:** Always-on percentage fee
**Growth driver:** Every other module generates credits that feed the marketplace

### Tier 4 — B2C + Grants + Index Licensing
Consumer impact subscriptions. UN/World Bank/foundation grants. VYRION Index data feed licensing to financial terminals. Academic data partnerships.

**Index licensing target:** $50K–$500K per institution per year (Bloomberg, Refinitiv, FactSet)
