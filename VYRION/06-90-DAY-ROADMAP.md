## THE THREE NON-NEGOTIABLE DAILY HABITS

**1. Publish the VYRION Index every day.** Five minutes. No exceptions. The consistency is the product.

**2. Engage every reply on CONSCIENCE posts.** Every person who challenges the data is a validator in the making. Respond with one line: *"Their own filing. Full sources at the link."*

**3. Add one company to CONSCIENCE per month.** Each new company is a new post, a new infographic, a new reason for a new audience to find the platform.
